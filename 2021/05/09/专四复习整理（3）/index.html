    It’s getting d江ker and darker. I am afraid a heavy rain is _____.

    解析

  19. 错选C
    解释说明wish，是同位语
  20. 错选D
    such a (noun)/many such …前位/后位限定词是根据位置顺序分的。
  21. 错选B
    when while on the contrary; considering that; whereas: e.g. Why are you here when you should be in school?
  22. 错选C
    on the rocks Destitute, bankrupt, e.g. Can I borrow next month’s rent? I’m on the rocks.
  23. 错选B
    fabricate to fake; forge (a document, signature, etc.).
    fake: to pretend; simulate: e.g. to fake illness.
  24. 错选C
    skid: to slip or slide sideways, as an automobile in turning a corner rapidly.
  25. 错选D
    that everyone wants to buy 是个定语从句，前面是such所以用as
  26. 错选D
    这里是个倒装，than a developed country does.
  27. 错选B
    finishing touches: he last thing done to make something complete e.g. He’s putting the finishing touches on his latest novel.
  28. 错选D
    B,D的as后面的从句是省略形式，而C后面要用her
  29. 错选D
    such that后面要用完整句子
  30. 错选C
    gloomy: hopeless or despairing; pessimistic, e.g. a gloomy view of the future.
    shady: abounding in shade; e.g., shady paths.
    shadowy:unsubstantial, unreal, or illusory, e.g. shadowy preoccupations.
  31. 错选A
    installment: any of several parts into which a debt or other sum payable is divided for payment at successive fixed times, e.g. to pay for furniture in monthly installments
  32. 错选A
    对现在的感受提问，不需要用过去完成时
  33. 错选A
    teach sth. to sb. sth. 是直接宾语
  34. 错选B
    project be started, 应该用被动
  35. 错选C
    wound更合适一些: an injury, usually involving division of tissue
  36. 错选C
    impede: to retard in movement or progress by means of obstacles or hindrances; obstruct; hinder. e.g. And, racial gaps also impeded access to testing and affected quality of care.
    impend: to be imminent; be about to happen. e.g. But, in this time of impending climate crisis, O’Rourke says that every little bit counts.
